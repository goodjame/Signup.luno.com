Why choose Luno?

Luno aims to be one of the most compliant crypto investment platforms in the world. We take a regulation-first approach and have some of the most intensive security processes in crypto, according to an April 2023 ranking by CCData.

We vet all cryptocurrencies and new features against Luno's internal due diligence standards before making them available, and provide straightforward information to help you understand the risks so you can build and execute an investment strategy that’s right for you.

Please note, Luno is not regulated in the UK and cryptoassets are high-risk investments that are largely unregulated.
